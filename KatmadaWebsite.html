Who am I?

So, why I am interested in becoming a web developer? I enjoy doing this—I get lost in it, and would not mind working for months on the same page, finessing little details. Is there anything else to be said, really? If only I knew about FAC earlier, so that I could work on my projects for more than the three days that I did. I will keep working on them regardless of the outcome of this application. So, what are my goals with this? My long term plan about software developing would be very ambitious—to create legaltech/fintech or other (not related to law) tools that completelly change the way people work or make transcactions, and make their lives more efficient and less worrisome. In the meantime, my other, perhaps still ambitious but more manageable goal, is to learn to develop websites and apps for all kinds of clients, and especially small scale companies and startups who support their local communities, as well as follow sustainability rules where possible. I am helping the owner of an e-shop/subscription box in Greece right now and would love to be able to redo their website, or any website really, from scratch.
